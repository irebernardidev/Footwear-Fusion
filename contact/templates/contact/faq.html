Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the delivery timeline for orders?

A: Your order will embark on its journey to you within 3-10 business days, if you're in Europe. This includes the time needed for both processing and shipping We strive to get your order to you as swiftly as possible.

Q: What is the process for returning an item?

A: We at Footwear Fusion prioritize your satisfaction and offer a 28-day return period for any items that don't meet your expectations, as long as they remain in sellable condition. To initiate a return, simply reach out to us via our contact form, mentioning your order number and the reason for the return. We'll respond with a return label for you. If our original packaging isn't available, any secure, waterproof packaging will do. You can even consolidate returns from multiple orders, just be sure to include all corresponding return notes in the parcel.

Q: How can I confirm my order was successfully placed?

A: To verify your order, simply log into your account and check your purchase history. If your order is listed there, rest assured we have received it and are in the process of getting it ready. If it's not visible in your purchase history, please don't hesitate to get in touch with us for further assistance.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We welcome payments through MasterCard, Visa, American Express, and Discovery. Your security is paramount at Footwear Fusion, and we ensure the utmost protection of your information through encrypted transmission, safeguarding any personal data.

Q: Is it possible to cancel or modify my order?

A: Once your order is confirmed, we move quickly to get it ready for shipment, which means we're unable to make changes or cancel it. However, if you contact us immediately after placing the order, we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

Still have questions? We're here to assist! Reach out to us for more information or help.
